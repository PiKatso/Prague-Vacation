Prague Castle is an age-old symbol of Czech lands that belongs among the most significant Czech sights, renowned as the seat of rulers and later also presidents of the Czech Republic. According to Guinness World Records, Prague Castle is the largest castle in the world. It is also listed on the UNESCO World Cultural Heritage List. During this tour you will visit the Old Royal Castle, St. George’s Basilica, St. Vitus Cathedral and the fairytale-like Golden Lane with Daliborka Tower and House No. 22, where author Franc Kafka lived. Our tour will finish under the Old Castle stairs, where the guide will say goodbye to you.
